About Us

Founded in 2010, Suzhou Xinyuseng Import and Export Co., Ltd. is a professional manufacturer and exporter of high-quality textile fabrics. We specialize in polyester, viscose, and functional fabrics for fashion, sportswear, and industrial applications.

Our products are exported to more than 30 countries including USA, Europe, Southeast Asia, and the Middle East. We maintain strict quality control standards and offer competitive pricing with reliable delivery.
